Interactive Test Scenario

Scenario: Imagine you get an urgent notice saying your account security is compromised. The message tells you to fix it immediately by clicking below:

http://secure-login-verification-portal-web.com/update-password

⚠️ Caught in the Trap!

This was a simulated phishing link.

In a real attack, clicking this could have silently downloaded malware or sent you to a fake login page designed to steal your credentials.

Rule #1: Always check the full sender address and URL before touching any link!
